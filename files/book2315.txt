与西方绘画相比，中国绘画别具一格，自成一统。本书在简要概述中国绘画艺术的基础上，重点对历代绘画名家名作进行分析、导读，带领读者通过具体作品，体悟中国绘画艺术的独到之美。Compared with the western painting, the Chinese painting has a unique style of its own. The book, based on a brief introduction to the Chinese painting art, lays emphasis on analysis of well-known works of famous artists of different dynasties, aiming to guide the readers to feel the unique beauty of the Chinese painting art through specific works.
